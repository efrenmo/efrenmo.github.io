About Me

I'm a data engineer with 2+ years of specialized experience architecting and implementing end-to-end ETL pipelines that process complex, large-scale datasets. My background spans financial services, startup environments, and cutting-edge data engineering across cloud platforms.

Through hands-on implementations, I've applied analytics engineering practices to streamline data modeling, improve transformation pipelines, and enable comprehensive business intelligence workflows.

My focus is on delivering scalable, maintainable data infrastructure that enables teams to make data-driven decisions with confidence. I combine deep technical expertise with strong collaboration skills to translate complex business requirements into robust cloud-based solutions.
